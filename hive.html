HIVE (8-bit MVP)

An 8-bit phone RPG where Scouts, Citizens, and Firekeepers explore the Dome, gather Thunder Shards, earn Relics, face Spirit Animal trials, and choose between Pure or Haste Crown paths.

Core Loop

Collect 7 Shards → Earn 3 Relics → Pass 3 Spirit Trials → Choose Path (Pure/Haste).

KYC Paths

Pure = 100% reveal after Cycle 300; Haste = 80% reveal + 20% burn at Cycle 500.

Legendary Rewards

Cycle 300 & 500 artifacts become on-chain collectibles (non-transferable badges + commemoratives).
